Buttered Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • butter
  • salt & pepper

How to make

  1. Boil the noodles in a pot (with water) for 5 minutes. Then drain the water through a colander, and put the the noodles back into the pot.
  2. Add salt & pepper and one tablespoon of butter to the pan.
  3. Mix the ingredients
